\section{Introduction}
\label{sec:intro}

A century after \citet{kitchin1923}, \citet{juglar1862},
\citet{kuznets1930} and \citet{kondratieff1925} introduced the four
canonical economic cycles, the periodicities they identified remain
widely cited and routinely invoked in econophysics
\citep{korotayev_tsirel2010}, macroprudential policy
\citep{borio2014,drehmann_borio_tsatsaronis2012}, and the long-wave
literature \citep{perez2002,modelski1987}. Yet four successive waves of
empirical scepticism---\citet{garvy1943} on Kondratieff,
\citet{klotz_neal1973} on Kuznets, \citet{solomou1987} on both,
\citet{romer1999} and \citet{stock_watson2003} on Juglar, and
\citet{wen2005} on Kitchin---have steadily eroded the claim that any of
these periodicities is more than an artefact of measurement, sample
selection or visual inspection. \citet{burns_mitchell1946} themselves
refused to commit to a canonical period when constructing the NBER
business-cycle dating procedure.

This paper closes the gap between the canonical claim and the empirical
consensus by applying a single pre-registered falsifiable protocol to
the strongest data and methods available. We treat the four cycles as
\emph{conjectures} in the Popperian sense, gather the most exhaustive
macroeconomic panel set assembled to date (1700--2024, six independent
sources), arm the conjectures with the best available cycle-fitting
machinery (Christiano-Fitzgerald \citep{christiano_fitzgerald2003},
Morlet wavelets \citep{torrence_compo1998}, Hamilton-switching
\citep{hamilton1989}, Bry-Boschan/Harding-Pagan
\citep{bry_boschan1971,harding_pagan2002} and PELT
\citep{killick_fearnhead_eckley2012}), and test them against a
conservative dual null \citep{theiler_et_al1992,schreiber_schmitz2000}.

Three contributions follow.

\paragraph{(i)~A three-gate falsification protocol, pre-registered.}
We submit every cell of the (cycle, panel, variable) grid to a cascade
of three gates---existence (Gate~1), method consensus (Gate~2) and
cross-panel universality (Gate~3)---whose thresholds were fixed before
data ingestion and frozen in source code
(\path{ecowave/cycles/bands.py:CYCLE_BANDS} is implemented as an
immutable \texttt{MappingProxyType}). A per-variable safeguard
\citep{wen2005} forbids composite aggregation artefacts. The protocol
is reviewed in detail in Appendix~\ref{app:criteria}.

\paragraph{(ii)~Maximum-charity for the cycles.}
We give the cycles every reasonable chance to survive. We use canonical
bands (Kitchin 3--5y, Juglar 7--11y, Kuznets 15--25y, Kondratieff
40--60y) frozen \emph{ex ante}, four independent estimators covering
band-pass, wavelet, regime-switching and changepoint families, and six
panels spanning 324 years and seventeen economies. The
Hodrick-Prescott filter \citep{hodrick_prescott1997,hamilton2018} and
empirical mode decomposition---both known to introduce spurious
periodicities---are excluded \emph{a priori} and the reasons are
documented in Appendix~\ref{app:methods}.

\paragraph{(iii)~Publication of every failure.}
Because the protocol pre-registers acceptance criteria, every cell
that fails is reported with the same prominence as those that pass.
Section~\ref{sec:results} summarises the result: across the
(cycle, panel, variable) grid, no cycle survives the full cascade on
more than a small minority of panels, Kondratieff is the most fragile,
and the few apparent survivals on income-aggregate composites
collapse under the per-variable safeguard---exactly the diagnosis of
\citet{wen2005} on inventory cycles.

The paper is deliberately short because its claim is narrow.
Section~\ref{sec:data} documents the data and their provenance.
Section~\ref{sec:methods} reviews the cycle-fitting arsenal.
Section~\ref{sec:criteria} formalises the three gates.
Section~\ref{sec:results} reports the verdict.
Section~\ref{sec:pointers} sketches an alternative empirical signature
and points to a companion methodological paper.
Section~\ref{sec:conclusion} concludes.

Appendices~\ref{app:data}--\ref{app:per_variable} carry the technical
detail needed to defend each pillar against a hostile reviewer:
provenance with SHA-256 checksums, formulae and implementation pointers,
the pre-registered acceptance criteria, and the per-variable evidence
that demonstrates composite-aggregation artefacts.

\paragraph{Thesis.}
Our central claim is twofold. \emph{Empirically:} no canonical cycle
survives a state-of-the-art pre-registered cascade on state-of-the-art
data. \emph{Methodologically:} this is not a failure of the protocol---it
is the quantitative confirmation, under a falsifiable design, of a
sceptical empirical consensus that has been building since
\citet{garvy1943}. Modelling of macroeconomic fluctuations should
move beyond the cycle paradigm and adopt the alternative statistical
signatures sketched in Section~\ref{sec:pointers}.
