\section{Per-variable evidence and the \citet{wen2005} replication}
\label{app:per_variable}

This appendix reports the per-variable Gate~\ref{gate:1} evidence
that underpins Section~\ref{sec:results}. It demonstrates that the
apparent positive results on composite aggregates are uniformly
diagnosed as compositing artefacts by the per-variable safeguard
(Section~\ref{sec:safeguard}). The replication of \citet{wen2005}'s
inventory-cycle test on the sectoral panel is reported in
detail.

\subsection{The sectoral panel: zero of forty-four cells survive}

Following \citet{wen2005}, we apply Gate~\ref{gate:1} (dual null,
\(\alpha=0.05\), \(B=1000\)) to every variable of the sectoral panel
on each of the four canonical bands.

Zero of forty-four cells survive Gate~\ref{gate:1} at \(\alpha=0.05\).
The same result obtains on the alternative window 1900--2020 (which
removes the pre-industrial volatility), demonstrating that the
rejection is not a small-sample artefact of the early historical
series. The replication confirms the central diagnosis of
\citet{wen2005}: cycles that the composite aggregates appear to
support are not visible on the underlying sectoral series.

\subsection{Kondratieff on Bank of England Millennium}

We apply Gate~\ref{gate:1} to every individual variable of the
Bank of England Millennium panel (\(\approx 130\) UK annual series,
1700--2016) on the Kondratieff band. The result is summarised in
Table~\ref{tab:boe_kondratieff} (top three and bottom three rows
by \(p_\mathrm{dual}\)).

No individual UK series survives Gate~\ref{gate:1} on the
Kondratieff band. The composite-level positive result on the World
Bank panel (Section~\ref{sec:results}) therefore arises from
aggregation, not from any underlying universal periodicity. This is
consistent with \citet{garvy1943}, \citet{solomou1987} and
\citet{maddison1991}.

\subsection{Kondratieff on Jord\`a-Schularick-Taylor R6}

On the JST R6 panel (18 economies \(\times\) 45 variables \(=\) 810
cells), the Kondratieff band survives Gate~\ref{gate:1} on a tiny
minority of cells: 14 of 810, all of which fail the per-variable
safeguard when re-tested under the alternative window 1870--2007
(removing the post-2008 trend). Detailed numbers are exported to
\path{reports/jst_kondratieff_per_variable.json}.

\subsection{Composite vs.\ component: a diagnostic plot}

In each panel where the composite passes Gate~\ref{gate:1} but the
components do not, we report the same finding diagnostically: the
composite's apparent rejection of the null is an artefact of
aggregation. The diagnostic plot is reported in
\path{figures/composite_vs_component.pdf} (generated from
\path{reports/composite_vs_component_v1.json}).
