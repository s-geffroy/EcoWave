# Dow/Elliott adaptation to systemic crises

## Principle

Dow identifies structural regimes. Elliott is only a candidate grammar for collective acceleration, panic, correction and over-extension.

## Non-negotiable constraint

A wave does not exist because the narrative is elegant. It exists only if independent curves confirm a phase change.

## V1 curves

- E — economic / financial stress
- D — diplomatic / institutional stress
- S — social / regime stress
- L — logistics / energy stress
- I — information / narrative stress

Military/security `M` is not central in the 2008 pilot and must not be forced.
